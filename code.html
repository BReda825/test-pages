Tom Aspinall

Biography

Tom Aspinall is a professional mixed martial artist hailing from the United Kingdom. Born on April 4, 1993, Aspinall has made a name for himself in the world of MMA, particularly in the heavyweight division. Aspinall began his professional career in 2014 and has since been on an impressive run. He has competed in various promotions, including BAMMA and Cage Warriors, before signing with the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) in 2020. Known for his powerful striking and quick finishes, Aspinall has won the majority of his fights by knockout. His technical skills, combined with his athleticism and determination, have made him a formidable opponent in the octagon. Off the mat, Aspinall is known for his humble demeanor and dedication to the sport. He is a family man, often sharing his love for his family during interviews. His journey serves as an inspiration to many aspiring fighters around the world.
